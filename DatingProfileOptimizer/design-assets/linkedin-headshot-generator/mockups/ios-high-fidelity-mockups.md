## iOS-Specific Design Principles Applied:

### Typography Hierarchy:
- **Large Title**: SF Pro Display 34pt (Navigation bars)
- **Title 1**: SF Pro Display 28pt (Main headings)
- **Title 2**: SF Pro Display 22pt (Section headings)
- **Headline**: SF Pro Text 17pt Semibold (Buttons, labels)
- **Body**: SF Pro Text 17pt Regular (Main content)
- **Subheadline**: SF Pro Text 15pt Regular (Secondary content)
- **Footnote**: SF Pro Text 13pt Regular (Captions, metadata)

### iOS Color System:
- **Label**: #000000 (Primary text)
- **Secondary Label**: #3C3C43 (99% black, 60% opacity)
- **Tertiary Label**: #3C3C43 (99% black, 30% opacity)
- **System Background**: #FFFFFF (Main background)
- **Secondary System Background**: #F2F2F7 (Grouped content)

### Professional App Characteristics:
- **Trustworthy**: Clean design with professional color palette
- **Efficient**: Minimal steps to achieve professional results
- **High-quality**: Focus on premium output and user experience
- **LinkedIn-ready**: Direct integration and usage guidance
- **ROI-focused**: Clear career advancement value proposition

### iOS Native Components:
- **Navigation Controllers**: Standard iOS navigation patterns
- **Activity Indicators**: Native iOS loading states
- **Share Sheets**: iOS native sharing functionality
- **Photo Picker**: iOS Photos app integration
- **Haptic Feedback**: Appropriate tactile responses
- **Gesture Recognizers**: Pinch, pan, and tap gestures
- **Safe Area Layout**: Proper handling of device-specific layouts

This creates a premium, professional iOS application that feels native to the platform while delivering clear career advancement value through AI-powered headshot generation.
