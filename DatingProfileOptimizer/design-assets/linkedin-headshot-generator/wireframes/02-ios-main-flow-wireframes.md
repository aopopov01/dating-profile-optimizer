## Design Principles - iOS Professional Flow:

### iOS Native Patterns:
1. **Navigation Controllers**: Standard push/pop navigation
2. **Activity Indicators**: Native iOS loading spinners
3. **Action Sheets**: iOS-style selection menus
4. **Share Extensions**: Native iOS sharing functionality
5. **Safe Area Layout**: Proper handling of notch/home indicator

### Professional UX:
1. **Guided Experience**: Step-by-step professional setup
2. **Quality Control**: Clear photo requirements and validation
3. **Progress Transparency**: Clear processing steps and timing
4. **Results Focus**: Immediate before/after comparisons
5. **Integration Ready**: Direct LinkedIn and resume usage

### Conversion Elements:
1. **Social Proof**: Professional statistics and benefits
2. **Immediate Value**: Quick results and easy download
3. **Usage Guidance**: Clear next steps for career impact
4. **Quality Assurance**: Professional standards and guarantees
5. **Viral Features**: Easy sharing and referral options

### iOS Design Excellence:
1. **Typography Hierarchy**: Proper SF Pro usage
2. **Color Psychology**: Professional blues with trust indicators
3. **Touch Interactions**: Proper 44pt touch targets
4. **Accessibility**: VoiceOver support and high contrast
5. **Performance**: Smooth animations and responsive interactions
