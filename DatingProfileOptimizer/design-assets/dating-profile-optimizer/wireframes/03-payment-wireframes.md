## Design Principles for Payment Flow:

### Trust & Security:
1. **SSL Badges**: Clear security indicators
2. **Familiar Payment Methods**: Major cards, PayPal, Apple Pay
3. **Transparent Pricing**: No hidden fees shown upfront
4. **Money-Back Guarantee**: Risk reversal for user confidence

### Conversion Optimization:
1. **Social Proof**: "Most Popular" badges and testimonials
2. **Urgency**: Limited-time offers and discounts
3. **Value Stacking**: Clear benefit comparisons
4. **Objection Handling**: FAQ and support options

### User Experience:
1. **Progressive Disclosure**: Payment details revealed gradually
2. **Auto-fill**: Support for saved payment methods
3. **Error Handling**: Clear validation messages
4. **Receipt Confirmation**: Immediate success feedback

### Retention Strategy:
1. **Usage Tracking**: Show value delivered
2. **Win-back Offers**: Discounts for canceling users
3. **Feedback Collection**: Understand churn reasons
4. **Feature Previews**: Tease upcoming premium features

### Android Material Design:
1. **Cards**: Elevated pricing comparison cards
2. **Primary Actions**: Prominent upgrade buttons
3. **Typography**: Clear pricing hierarchy
4. **Icons**: Material Design payment icons
5. **Form Fields**: Proper Material text field styling
